Scrambled Eggs

Description

Learn how to make scrambled eggs

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Olive Oil
  • Ham
  • Frozen Corn
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Salt and Pepper

Directions

  1. Apply olive oil to frying pan
  2. Add frozen corn to pan
  3. Add eggs and scramble them
  4. Add ham
  5. Add mozzarella cheese
  6. Add salt and pepper
